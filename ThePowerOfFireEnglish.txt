Run Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne to play the modification.
Go to "Singleplayer" - "Campaigns" and play the singleplayer campaign after the game has been started.
Choose one of the maps in the folder "The Power of Fire" for multiplayer games.

Uninstall the modification or remove the file "War3Mod.mpq" from the directory of Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne to disable the modification.
The uninstaller is in the Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne directory.
Uninstalling the modification won't remove your savegames.
